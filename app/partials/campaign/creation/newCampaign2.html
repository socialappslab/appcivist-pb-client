Campaign Milestones represent the concrete steps the assembly follow to achieve the goal of the campaign. Setup the starting date of each milestone of your campaign by using the slider below.

  1. Each handle in the slider represents a milestone. Slide them in the timeline to change their starting dates.
  2. The separation between circles represents the duration (in days).
  3. Below the timeline, each milestones is described by number, with its corresponding starting date and duration according to their positions in the timeline.
  4. Milestones are organized within each component used in the campaign, as shown in the summary.
